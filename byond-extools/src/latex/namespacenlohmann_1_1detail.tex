the J\+S\+ON type enumeration 

This enumeration collects the different J\+S\+ON types. It is internally used to distinguish the stored values, and the functions \mbox{\hyperlink{classnlohmann_1_1basic__json_afe9a6fe72337a174cfb117a2081d2cfc}{basic\+\_\+json\+::is\+\_\+null()}}, \mbox{\hyperlink{classnlohmann_1_1basic__json_ae3300a95d3bb4a55cb0e04b9f125e42f}{basic\+\_\+json\+::is\+\_\+object()}}, \mbox{\hyperlink{classnlohmann_1_1basic__json_a1859e0c783a24df797b758e3fc21b966}{basic\+\_\+json\+::is\+\_\+array()}}, \mbox{\hyperlink{classnlohmann_1_1basic__json_ae958b0bc067c82850145299711d8bcd5}{basic\+\_\+json\+::is\+\_\+string()}}, \mbox{\hyperlink{classnlohmann_1_1basic__json_a0bd23e6f679145905a78020c07547559}{basic\+\_\+json\+::is\+\_\+boolean()}}, \mbox{\hyperlink{classnlohmann_1_1basic__json_a5d5f20be10c6f6d263557def2ac504a3}{basic\+\_\+json\+::is\+\_\+number()}} (with \mbox{\hyperlink{classnlohmann_1_1basic__json_a145005bad4fcab4f8620efec8de019ad}{basic\+\_\+json\+::is\+\_\+number\+\_\+integer()}}, \mbox{\hyperlink{classnlohmann_1_1basic__json_ac45bc02fd5f4f136f522844222012adc}{basic\+\_\+json\+::is\+\_\+number\+\_\+unsigned()}}, and \mbox{\hyperlink{classnlohmann_1_1basic__json_a926df0038ca14568f88a92d0caeba044}{basic\+\_\+json\+::is\+\_\+number\+\_\+float()}}), \mbox{\hyperlink{classnlohmann_1_1basic__json_a4fce66d66c424052b192ba45e08a661d}{basic\+\_\+json\+::is\+\_\+discarded()}}, \mbox{\hyperlink{classnlohmann_1_1basic__json_a422885c85f603a13c36f8d0ac5d9f278}{basic\+\_\+json\+::is\+\_\+primitive()}}, and \mbox{\hyperlink{classnlohmann_1_1basic__json_a5a53e5a09d9123bfeed6f664b1334f8c}{basic\+\_\+json\+::is\+\_\+structured()}} rely on it.

\end{DoxyNote}
